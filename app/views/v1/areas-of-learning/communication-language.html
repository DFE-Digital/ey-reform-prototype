Communication and languageOverview

These pages explore the communication and language learning area of the EYFS. They will help you to plan your curriculum to best support children’s learning and meet EYFS standards.

As you plan your curriculum, consider what you want children to learn first. Then, make sure you plan activities and opportunities to help them achieve this.
